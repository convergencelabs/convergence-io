Convergence is provided as a collection of enterprise-ready containers for easy installation in your own on-premise or cloud environment.

Annual license fees are negotiated per customer. The price is anchored on the average monthly number of Convergence sessions. A session represents a single websocket connection to Convergence, during which any of our APIs are available.

Convergence licenses are available on a case-by-case basis. Whether you're a bootstrapped startup or large enterprise, we can work out a license fee that gets everyone's needs met. To get a custom quote, fill out this form to speak to someone on our sales team.
